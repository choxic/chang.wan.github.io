Hi! I am a Ph.D. candidate in the College of Media and International Culture at Zhejiang University, supervised by Dr. Yingru Ji. I am currently a visiting student at the Annenberg School for Communication, University of Southern California, advised by Dr. Angel Hsing-Chi Hwang.

My research centers on three interconnected areas: (1) social experience and social dynamics in human–AI interaction, with a particular focus on attributional processes; (2) AI-mediated communication and its influence on interpersonal communication; and (3) AI for social good, with an emphasis on corporate social responsibility (CSR) communication in the age of AI.

Always on the lookout for curious, thoughtful collaborators. If you'd like to connect or explore something interesting together, feel free to reach out.
